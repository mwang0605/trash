River Trail Middle School E-Sports Club

For years, computer games has been considered as nothing but a mere waste of time, and still many people belive it is. The lack of any physical need has led many to believe that playing computer games could never become anything but a time passer. Today, we stand together to show the world that gaming is more than just a hobby. E-Sports have begun to create waves across North America, and River Trail has taken notice. As River Trail Wolves, we have taken initiative to make it possible for our fellow students a chance to make pave their path into something unique. Our staff is filled with people who are passionate enough to dedicate time out of their school year to help make this dream possible.
